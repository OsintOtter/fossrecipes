## Ingredients

* 1/2 tsp Salt
* 1 tbsp Cumin
* 1 tsp Oregano
* 1 tsp Garlic
* 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
* 1/4 tsp Paprika
* 1 dash of chilli powder (optional)

## Directions
Mix together. Adjust quantities to taste.

Seasons 1lb of meat.
